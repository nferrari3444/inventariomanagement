Application to manage logistic and inventory operations for an uruguayan company.

The application was built with the Django Framework and contains functionalities such as login, register, authentication, authorization, send emails to confirm inventory movements 
as well as purchases and sales of products. 

Provide information of inventory of products and manage operations between warehouses regards the states of the transfers such as "Pending", "In Transit", "Confirmed".
Also provide information about stock security, and has alerts over products that are below they stock security.
